## What Gusto Actually Does

Gusto is not just payroll software. It's a full HR platform that happens to be built around payroll. The three core pillars:

1. **Payroll** — calculate wages, withhold taxes, direct deposit, file quarterly and annual tax forms
2. **Benefits** — health insurance, 401(k), FSA/HSA, workers' comp, commuter benefits
3. **HR** — onboarding, time tracking, PTO, org chart, performance tools, compliance alerts

Most small businesses start with payroll and layer in benefits and HR tools as they grow.

## Payroll Features

### Automatic Tax Filing

This is Gusto's most important feature for small businesses. Gusto calculates, withholds, and *files* your payroll taxes automatically:

- Federal income tax withholding (employees)
- Social Security and Medicare (employer + employee)
- Federal unemployment tax (FUTA)
- State income tax withholding (all 50 states)
- State unemployment insurance (SUI)
- Local taxes where applicable

At year end, Gusto automatically generates and files W-2s for employees and 1099s for contractors. You don't do anything except verify the numbers are right.

For a business owner who's been manually doing quarterly 941s or paying an accountant to do it, this alone justifies the cost.

### Payroll Processing

Running payroll takes about 5–10 minutes. You approve hours, review the payroll summary, and click submit. Funds come out of your bank 2 days before payday (4-day processing) or 1 day before (next-day on Plus plan).

Off-cycle payroll runs (bonuses, commissions, termination checks) are included at no extra cost. This matters — competitors sometimes charge per off-cycle run.

### Multiple Pay Schedules

You can run different employees on different schedules — weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, monthly. Common if you have some salaried employees (bi-weekly) and some hourly (weekly).

### Contractor Payments

Pay 1099 contractors through Gusto alongside W-2 employees. Gusto handles 1099-NEC filing automatically. This is included in all plans.

## Benefits Administration

Gusto's benefits platform is genuinely impressive for a small business tool. You get:

**Health Insurance:** Gusto acts as a licensed insurance broker in all 50 states. You can shop and enroll employees in health, dental, and vision coverage directly through Gusto. Plans from major carriers (Aetna, UnitedHealth, Kaiser, and regional carriers). Gusto handles employee enrollment, life event changes, and annual renewals.

**401(k):** Gusto has its own 401(k) product (Gusto Financial) with SIMPLE IRAs and 401(k) plans. Contributions deduct automatically from payroll. Employer matching is configured once and deducted automatically.

**FSA/HSA:** Flexible and health spending accounts available. Employee contributions are pre-tax and deducted automatically from payroll.

**Workers' Comp:** Pay-as-you-go workers' comp through Next Insurance (no large upfront premium payment — you pay per payroll based on actual wages).

**Commuter Benefits:** Pre-tax transit and parking benefits.

The benefit administration integrates directly with payroll — deductions and employer contributions are calculated and deducted automatically. You don't reconcile benefits invoices manually.

## HR Features

### Digital Onboarding

When you hire someone new, Gusto sends them an onboarding checklist:
- W-4 (federal tax withholding)
- State withholding form
- I-9 identity verification
- Direct deposit setup
- Benefits enrollment
- Offer letter (if you upload one)
- Employee handbook acknowledgment

New hires complete everything themselves before their first day. You don't handle paperwork.

### PTO Tracking

Set up PTO policies (accrual rates, carryover rules, waiting periods). Employees can see their balance and request time off. Managers approve or deny. All of it syncs to payroll automatically (PTO is paid automatically when taken).

### Time Tracking (Plus Plan)

Employees clock in/out via the Gusto app or web. Hours flow directly into payroll — no manual data entry. The time tracking also handles overtime calculations automatically (federal and state overtime rules).

### Compliance Alerts

Gusto monitors federal and state labor law changes and sends you alerts when action is required. New minimum wage laws, new leave requirements, ACA compliance — Gusto watches these so you don't have to.

### Employee Self-Service Portal

Every employee gets a Gusto account where they can:
- View pay stubs and W-2s
- Update their address and personal information
- Change their W-4 withholding
- See their benefits coverage
- Request PTO

This eliminates a huge category of HR administrative questions.

## Customer Support
